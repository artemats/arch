The striking visual stance of the ARCH 1s is enhanced by the flow of the aerodynamically efficient fuel cell into the elegant sport-style tail section. The carbon fiber and aluminum bodywork allows for personalized livery and finishes, as with all ARCH motorcycles the ergonomic and aesthetic package are tailored to the owner.

Combining hand-assembled attention to detail, a uniquely aggressive visual profile and V-twin street sport performance the ARCH 1s is an evolution of the performance cruiser defying convention.
