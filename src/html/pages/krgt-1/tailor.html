Personalized ergonomics

Tailored to you

The hand-assembly of every KRGT-1 is a process that includes a bespoke ergonomic and aesthetic package. Tailoring the fit of the motorcycle to perfectly suit the rider gives a more intuitive riding experience and a better connection with the machine.

The bespoke ergonomic package is centered around a unique seat shape that is tailored for the rider. From here infinite adjustments in bar, foot peg, hand and foot control position create the perfect riding interface for the owner.
